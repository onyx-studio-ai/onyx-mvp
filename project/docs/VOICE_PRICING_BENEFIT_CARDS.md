# Voice Pricing Benefit Cards (Non-Numeric)

Use these cards for sales pages, pricing pages, and internal pitch alignment.

## Layer A: Fast Delivery
- **Who it is for:** teams that need speed and predictable turnaround.
- **What client gets:** production-ready voice output, clear delivery window, baseline revision scope.
- **Why it exists:** minimize time-to-publish for low-complexity scripts.
- **Upgrade trigger to Layer B:** brand continuity or recurring updates are required.
- **One-sentence pitch:** "Launch faster with reliable voice output and clear delivery expectations."

## Layer B: Traceable Voice Asset
- **Who it is for:** brands that need consistent voice across campaigns.
- **What client gets:** directed polish, traceable voice continuity, compatible patch workflow, auditable usage scope.
- **Why it exists:** protect long-term brand voice consistency and reduce re-casting risk.
- **Upgrade trigger to Layer C:** high-stakes campaigns need deeper performance control.
- **One-sentence pitch:** "Build a continuous brand voice that can be updated without starting over."

## Layer C: Human-Led Premium Recording
- **Who it is for:** projects where performance nuance and brand risk are mission-critical.
- **What client gets:** verified human performance, direction control, premium handling for critical deliverables.
- **Why it exists:** maximize confidence for high-visibility launches and sensitive brand use.
- **One-sentence pitch:** "Use verified human performance when precision and brand safety matter most."

| Dimension | Layer A | Layer B | Layer C |
