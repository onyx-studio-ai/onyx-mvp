# Onyx Studios — Talent Engagement Agreement

> **Status:** Template / Reference Document
> **Purpose:** This document serves as the legal framework for the formal contract between Onyx Studios and its Talent. It is intended to be used as a PDF/offline agreement issued after Admin approval.
> **Last Updated:** February 22, 2026
> **ToS Cross-Reference:** Sections 54-58 of the Terms of Service

## Part I: Basic Terms

### 1. Parties & Recitals

- **Party A:** Fine Entertainment | 凡音文化有限公司, d/b/a Onyx Studios (Unified Business No.: 24312593)
- **Party B:** [Talent Full Legal Name]
- **Recital:** Talent possesses professional vocal performance capabilities. Onyx operates an AI-powered audio production platform. Both parties agree to collaborate under the following terms.

### 2. Definitions

- **"AI Twin"** — The AI voice model trained by Onyx using Training Materials provided by the Talent.
- **"Training Materials"** — Original audio recordings made by the Talent specifically for Onyx's AI model training purposes.
- **"Asset"** — Any digital audio file generated using the AI Twin or produced by the Talent under this Agreement.
- **"Micro-Patch"** — A request for the Talent to re-record a specific segment of an AI-generated Asset with their natural voice.
- **"License Certificate"** — The formal authorization document issued by Onyx to its Clients, documenting the specific rights granted.
- **"Archive Status"** — A model state in which the AI Twin ceases commercial generation but is retained for license validation purposes.
- **"Platform"** — The Onyx Studios website, API, and all associated tools and services at www.onyxstudios.ai.

### 3. Engagement Type

- Talent is engaged as an **Independent Contractor**, not an employee.
- All approved work is performed on a **Work Made for Hire** basis.
- This Agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, or employer-employee relationship.

### 4. Term

- **Initial Term:** From the date of execution for a period of [12 / 24] months.
- **Auto-Renewal:** Unless either party provides 30 days' written notice prior to expiration, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of equal length.
- **Immediate Termination Events:** Material breach, illegal conduct, or actions causing serious harm to the Onyx brand reputation.

## Part II: AI Twin Program (Path A Only)

### 5. Training Data Obligation

- Talent agrees to provide a minimum of **1 hour** of clean, home-studio quality audio.
- Content to be recorded based on scripts provided by Onyx.
- Onyx bears all model training costs; Talent is not charged any fee.
- Delivery standard: Dry audio, no background noise, 48kHz/24bit.

### 6. Exclusive AI License

- Talent grants Onyx the **exclusive** right to manage and commercialize their AI Twin.
- Talent **shall not** license the following to any third-party platform:
  - (a) Training Materials recorded specifically for Onyx
  - (b) The AI Twin model trained by Onyx, or any derivative thereof
- **Reservation of Rights:** Talent retains the right to use their natural voice to create entirely new recordings on other platforms or in other engagements. This exclusivity applies solely to Onyx's Training Materials and the resulting AI Twin model, and does not constitute a general non-compete restriction on the Talent's vocal identity.
- **Scope:** This Section applies only to Talent who have elected to participate in the AI Twin Program (Path A). Talent engaged solely under Traditional Studio Recording (Path B) are not bound by this Section.

### 7. AI Model Weights & IP Ownership

- Talent **retains** their natural vocal identity and Right of Publicity.
- Onyx **owns**:
  - (a) All AI model weights
  - (b) Training pipeline and algorithms
  - (c) All related source code and technical infrastructure
- Talent shall not, during or after the term of this Agreement, request extraction, copying, reverse engineering, or delivery of model weights.
- Clients who purchase licenses from Onyx acquire a **commercial use license**, not ownership of the model itself.

### 8. Royalty

- Onyx shall pay the Talent a royalty of **10%** of the final sale price for each pure-AI generation produced primarily using the Talent's AI Twin.
- **"Pure-AI generation"** means an Asset where the Talent's AI Twin model is the primary generation source.
- Royalties are calculated on a monthly basis, with settlement on the last business day of each month and payment by the 31st of the following month.
- Payment method: Bank transfer / PayPal / [to be specified].
- Onyx shall provide monthly statements detailing generation counts and corresponding amounts.
- **Tax responsibility:** Talent is solely responsible for tax reporting and payment obligations in their jurisdiction.

## Part III: Service Obligations

### 9. Micro-Patch Protocol

- Clients may request the Talent to re-record specific segments of AI-generated content with their natural voice.
- Rates:
  - **Voice Actor:** $10 USD per request
  - **Singer (up to 4 bars):** $15 USD per request
- Talent shall deliver within **48 hours** of receiving the request under normal circumstances.
- **Unavailability Notice:** Talent must notify Onyx within 24 hours of any period of unavailability (including illness, vacation, or personal leave).
- **Short-Term Absence (up to 14 days):** The 48-hour delivery deadline is suspended for the duration of the absence. Pending requests will be fulfilled upon the Talent's return. This does not constitute a breach of this Agreement.
- **Extended Absence (over 14 days):** Onyx reserves the right to fulfill pending Micro-Patch requests using an AI-based alternative or a substitute Talent with similar vocal characteristics. The original Talent will not be compensated for requests fulfilled by a substitute during their absence.
- This obligation exists only during the active term of this Agreement.
- Onyx is responsible for collecting payment from the Client and disbursing the Talent's share.

### 10. Traditional Recording Rates (Path B or Hybrid)

- Lead Vocal: $[X] USD per track
- Hook/Chorus: $[X] USD per track
- Additional rates per the Talent's rate card submitted at the time of application.
- All traditional recording work is performed on a Work Made for Hire basis.

## Part IV: Rights Transfer

### 11. Rights Transfer

- All approved works (including AI-generated and live-recorded) are **irrevocably** transferred to Onyx:
  - (a) Master Recording Rights
  - (b) Composition Rights (limited to portions created by the Talent)
  - (c) Neighboring Rights
- Transfer takes effect upon Onyx's approval of the work.
- This transfer is **perpetual and irrevocable**.

### 12. Moral Rights Waiver

- Talent **irrevocably** agrees not to assert any moral rights against Onyx or its authorized licensees, including:
  - (a) Right of Attribution
  - (b) Right of Integrity
- Clients are free to edit, remix, adapt, compress, or incorporate the work into derivative works.

### 13. Voice ID Affidavit

- Talent agrees to provide a **10-second Voice ID recording**.
- Content: An oral statement confirming identity and the lawful transfer of rights.
- The Voice ID will be linked to all License Certificates issued by Onyx for the Talent's works.
- Recorded once, used permanently.

## Part V: Termination & Departure

### 14. Termination

- Either party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days'** written notice.
- Grounds for immediate termination:
  - Material breach not cured within 15 days of written notice
  - Violation of law
  - Conduct causing serious damage to Onyx's brand reputation
- Termination does not affect rights and obligations accrued prior to the effective termination date.

### 15. Transition Period

- From the effective date of termination notice, a **30-day transition period** begins.
- During the transition period, the Talent shall complete all pending Micro-Patch requests.
- After the transition period, Onyx will not assign new Micro-Patch requests to the Talent.
- For post-transition client needs, Onyx will provide an AI alternative or assign a substitute Talent with similar vocal characteristics.

### 16. Non-Retroactive Departure

- Termination of this Agreement **shall not affect** any License Certificates issued prior to the effective termination date.
- All legacy Assets remain **fully and perpetually licensed** to existing Clients.
- The Talent irrevocably agrees not to make any claim, demand, or legal action against Onyx Studios or its Clients regarding such Assets.
- All Assets generated using the Talent's AI Twin prior to the termination date are deemed completed Works Made for Hire, unaffected by termination.

> **Suggested clause:** "Termination of this Agreement shall not affect any perpetual licenses already granted to third-party Clients prior to the effective termination date. The Talent irrevocably agrees that all legacy Assets remain fully and perpetually licensed to such Clients, and shall not make any claim, demand, or legal action against Onyx Studios or its Clients regarding such Assets."

### 17. Post-Departure Archival Rights

- Upon termination, the Talent's AI Twin model enters **Archive Status**:
  - (a) No longer used for new commercial generation
  - (b) No longer available for new client orders
  - (c) Retained for validation of existing License Certificates
  - (d) Retained for responding to copyright inquiries or legal disputes
- Archival is **permanent** (no destruction deadline).
- Legal basis: GDPR Article 6(1)(f) — Legitimate Interest (validating existing licenses).

### 18. Legacy Protection

- This Agreement is binding upon the Talent's heirs, executors, administrators, and assigns.
- Clients are protected against any claims from the Talent's estate or family members regarding right of publicity or post-mortem likeness rights in generated Assets.

## Part VI: General Terms

### 19. Confidentiality

- Talent shall not disclose Onyx's technical details, model architecture, training methods, or client information.
- This obligation survives termination for a period of **3 years**.

### 20. Non-Solicitation

- During the term and for **24 months** thereafter, the Talent shall not directly or indirectly solicit Onyx's Clients.
- Talent shall not circumvent the Onyx Studios Platform to engage privately with Clients introduced through Onyx.

### 21. Non-Disparagement

- During and after the term, neither party shall make defamatory or disparaging remarks about the other party.

### 22. Representations & Warranties

The Talent represents and warrants that:
- (a) All submitted audio features their own natural voice and does not contain synthetic generation or voice cloning.
- (b) Submissions do not infringe upon any third-party intellectual property rights.
- (c) Talent is at least **18 years of age** and possesses full legal capacity.
- (d) Talent has the right to enter into this Agreement and is not restricted by any other contractual obligation.
- (e) Talent is not subject to international sanctions or export controls.

### 23. Indemnification

- Talent shall indemnify and hold Onyx harmless from any third-party claims arising from breach of the Talent's representations or warranties.

### 24. Limitation of Liability

- Onyx's cumulative liability to the Talent is limited to the **total royalties paid to the Talent in the preceding 12 months**.
- Excludes indirect, incidental, or punitive damages.

### 25. Data Protection

- Onyx warrants that Talent's raw biometric data (including voice recordings, Voice ID, and electronic signatures) will be used solely for AI model generation and identity verification, and will not be shared with unauthorized third parties.
- Onyx processes Talent's personal data in accordance with its Privacy Policy.
- Compliant with Taiwan PDPA, GDPR, and applicable international data protection laws.
- Talent has the right to request access to and correction of personal data (model archival rights are not affected).

### 26. Taxes

- Talent is solely responsible for tax reporting and payment on royalty income.
- Onyx does not withhold taxes unless required by local law.

### 27. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

- Governed by the laws of **Taiwan (Republic of China)**.
- Exclusive jurisdiction: **New Taipei District Court**.
- International Talent: Good-faith negotiation for 30 days before court proceedings.

### 28. Severability

- If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full force and effect.

### 29. Entire Agreement

- This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
- No oral statements, WhatsApp/WeChat messages, or prior correspondence shall have legal effect.
- Amendments require written consent of both parties.

### 30. Execution

- Signatures of both parties + Date.
- Electronic signatures are legally equivalent to physical signatures under the Electronic Signatures Act of Taiwan (R.O.C.).
