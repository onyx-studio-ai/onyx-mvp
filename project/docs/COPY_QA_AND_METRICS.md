# Copy QA and Validation Metrics

## Scope
- Public customer pages only: home, service landing pages, create/order flows, checkout.
- Locales: `zh-TW`, `en`, `zh-CN`.

## QA Pass Checklist
- First screen clearly states service type, differentiation, deliverable, and next action.
- Terminology is consistent for `Content Use`, `License Scope`, `Plan`, and `Service Category`.
- Pricing units are consistent across page sections (especially voice tiers and checkout).
- License and tax wording is unambiguous (billing identity vs license recipient identity).
- Desktop and tablet text wrapping remains readable for hero and checkout key messages.

## 5-Second Clarity Test
- Show each first screen for 5 seconds to internal reviewer or target customer.
- Ask exactly three questions:
  1. What service is this page selling?
  2. Is this AI-only, human-only, or AI + human service?
  3. What should you click next?
- Pass criteria: at least 80% of testers answer all three correctly.

## Funnel Metrics (Before/After Copy Update)
- Hero CTA click-through rate.
- Service page to create-order page entry rate.
- Checkout abandonment rate.
- Support messages tagged with "not sure what this service is".

## Suggested 14-Day Observation Window
- Days 1-3: baseline snapshot (pre-change or early post-change).
- Days 4-10: monitor daily trend and anomaly spikes.
- Days 11-14: compare with baseline and summarize impact by locale.
